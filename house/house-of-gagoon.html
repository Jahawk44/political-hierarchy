House of Cassio is one of the most powerful noble houses in the Styx Empire. With Zeurkur Aproxima as its current representative, House Cassio is known for its vast wealth and resources. Situated in the southern part of the main continent "Continenta", House Cassio holds a staggering 80% of the Empire's gold and ore supplies, making them the largest supplier of these valuable resources. The immense wealth and power of House Cassio has made them a force to be reckoned with, and their influence in the Empire's political and economic landscape is undeniable. Despite its current representative, Zeurkur Aproxima, becoming increasingly greedy and isolated over time, the house remains a force to be reckoned with.

In addition to their wealth and power, House Cassio is also known for its trading practices, especially in the area of Servus, or contract servants. Yeetus Boximau, the younger brother of Zeurkur, is a well-known trader of Servus and is known for his expertise in this area. House Cassio is therefore a major player in the Servus trade, which is a significant source of income for the empire.

Overall, House Cassio is a respected and influential noble house in the Styx Empire, known for its wealth, power, and business acumen. Despite the eccentricities and greed of its current representative, Zeurkur, House Cassio continues to play a key role in the affairs of the empire.

Notable House Member

Zeurgeud Abadi


Zeurgeud Abadi is the older brother of Yeetus Bosimau and the representative of House Cassio in the Styx Empire. He is the owner of a massive gold mine in the south of the empire, which has made him the richest noble in the Styx Empire. Despite his wealth, Zeurgeud's reputation has changed over time. In the past, he was known for his generosity, but as his wealth grew, he became more greedy and isolated. He no longer interacts with the other nobles in the same way he once did, and many believe that he has become treacherous and cunning in his dealings. Despite this change in personality, Zeurgeud remains one of the most powerful figures in the Styx Empire and is respected for his wealth and influence.
