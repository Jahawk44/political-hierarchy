The House of Cellesela was one of the most renowned houses in the Styx Empire, renowned for their beautiful gardens, stunning architecture, and their skill in falconry. The House of Cellesela was known for their use of a goose-like bird native to their Cellesela lands, and their use of the birds' feathers to create the most effective arrows in the Styx Empire. This made the House of Cellesela renowned for their archers, and their mastery of the bow was feared and respected by all who heard of it.

Creo Ascella was proud to be a part of this powerful and respected house, and he was determined to uphold the family name and the traditions of the House of Cellesela. Unlike his sister Octavia, who was known as a skilled archer and a fierce warrior, Creo was known for his ability to tame the birds used for falconry. He had a gentle touch and a way with the creatures that made him one of the most skilled falconers in the Styx Empire.

Luna Elysia was renowned for her knowledge of plants and her ability to cultivate the most beautiful gardens in the Styx Empire, while Alyna Cerrine was known for her artistry and her skill in creating stunning architecture. Together, the four siblings of the House of Stella were a powerful and influential family, respected by all who knew of them.

The House of Cellesela was a symbol of strength, beauty, and tradition in the Styx Empire, and Noble Creo Ascella was proud to be a part of it. With his gentle touch and his way with the birds, he was ready to continue the family's traditions and lead them to glory.

Alyna Cerrine


Lady Alyna Cerrine is the second eldest daughter of the Cellesela House family. As one of the Immortals, she is a controversial figure in the Styx Empire. Her reputation as a lustful noble sometimes harms her and the family as a whole, despite her struggle to control her impulses. Renowned for her skills in herbalism and gardening, her talent in these areas has led her to provide new and sought-after items in the Cellesela Provinces. Her passionate charm is also notable, though it is often overshadowed by her reckless hedonism at night. She always wears silk gloves to protect the skin beneath during her gardening sessions. Her obsession with fleeting passions blinds her to deeper connections. She collects lovers like exotic flowers—admiring them intensely, then forgetting them once the bloom fades. This leaves her isolated, though she’d never admit it.

The House of Gaius is renowned throughout the Styx Empire for its Majestic Mount Stables, which are home to some of the strongest and most magnificent steeds in the land. The stables are a symbol of the House's military might, and the Gaius family's long-standing tradition of breeding and training powerful heavy cavalry units. The horses raised in the stables are prized not only for their strength and speed, but also for their beauty and grace, making them highly sought after by other noble houses and military organizations throughout the empire.

The House of Gaius is also known for its militaristic culture, with a strong emphasis on discipline, combat training, and martial prowess. Members of the House are raised to be warriors and leaders, and they are expected to excel in both physical and strategic warfare. The House of Gaius is respected and feared by many of its vassals and neighbors, and it is known for its fierce loyalty to the Styx Empire and its unwavering commitment to maintaining the safety and security of its lands and people. With its powerful cavalry, skilled warriors, and military expertise, House Gaius is a formidable force in the Styx Empire, and it is widely regarded as one of the greatest and most influential houses in the land.

The emblem of House of Gaius features a majestic horse rearing up on its hind legs, with its mane and tail flowing in the wind. The horse is surrounded by a circle of gold, which symbolizes the wealth and power of the House. The background is a deep blue, representing the loyalty and bravery of the Gaius family. The emblem is often displayed on the shields and banners of House Gaius's heavy cavalry units, as well as on the walls of its castles and fortresses.

Emperor Ciro Alcyon, the son of Croton Alcyon, is a known figure in the Styx Empire. He is a family-oriented man and is said to have a strong bond with his loved ones. He is also known for his gullibility and eccentric nature, which sometimes leads him to impulsive decisions. Despite this, he is widely respected and known as "The Great Builder."

Ciro is credited with numerous construction projects that have greatly contributed to the growth and development of the empire. He has a great passion for building and is known to take personal interest in every project he initiates. His dedication to construction has earned him the nickname "The Great Builder."

Aside from his love for building, Ciro is also known for his love of adventure. He is often seen traveling to far-off lands, seeking new challenges and experiences. Despite his impulsive nature, he has always managed to come out on top, further cementing his reputation as a fearless and capable leader. With his combination of passion, dedication, and adventurous spirit, Emperor Ciro Alcyon is a true symbol of the Styx Empire's power and might.
