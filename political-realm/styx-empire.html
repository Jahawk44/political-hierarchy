Styx Empire

The Styx Empire is a powerful nation located on the main continent of "Continenta." It was founded by Croton Alycon and has grown over the years to become one of the dominant powers on the continent. The empire is currently ruled by the Trianite Dynasty, with Emperor Ciro Alcyon at its head.

The Styx Empire has vassalized the Lynx Kingdom and the Sciatic League, and three nobles manage its territories. This centralization of power has allowed the empire to exert its influence over a vast area, bringing many territories under its control.

One of the key strategies used by Croton Alycon in the early days of the empire was to offer land to anyone who could claim it. This resulted in a massive expansion of Styxian territory, as many people sought to claim land for themselves. Over time, however, the empire centralized its power, either by force, influence, or the sheer goal of defending itself from other powerful landholders.

One of the most significant events in the history of the Styx Empire was its conquest of the great kingdom of Malazaer, located on the second continent of "Colonia." The defeat of Malazaer allowed the Styx Empire to gain a foothold on this new continent and establish its dominance over a vast area. Although the former kingdom of Malazaer is now exiled to the northern reaches of Colonia, it remains a shadow of its former glory.

Today, the Styx Empire is a symbol of civilization and power on the main continent of Continenta. Its centralization of power and successful expansion into Colonia have secured its place as one of the dominant powers in the world.
